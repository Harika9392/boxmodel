Healthy Foods

MILK

Milk is a white liquid food produced by the mammary glands of mammals. It is the primary source of nutrition.

EGGS

Eggs provide protein, fat, and important vitamins while being relatively low in calories.

CHICKEN

Chicken makes a fantastic substitute for red meats. A great source of protein, the meat has been linked to a variety of health benefits.

FISH

Fish is among the healthiest foods on the planetIt’s loaded with important nutrients, such as protein and vitamin D.

KEERA

Keera may provide you with Vitamin B1, Vitamin B2, Vitamin B3, Vitamin B5, Vitamin B6, Folic Acid, Vitamin C, Calcium,and Zinc.

CARROT

Carrots are a particularly good source of beta-carotene, fiber, vitamin K1, potassium, and antioxidants. Plus, they’re low in calories.

BEETROOT

Beets are particularly rich in folate, a vitamin that plays a key role in growth, development, and heart health.

FRUITS

Healthcare professionals know that fruit is a highly nutritious, delicious, and convenient food to any diet.
